Paul Schydlo

"What I cannot create, I do not understand." - R. Feynman

The human mind is a mysterious aparatus. Is it made up of discrete, interacting components or an emergent phenomena ruled by simple laws? Or something completely different?

I am fascinated by the thought of creating a being which is able to naturally interact with humans and our surroundings. Unlocking the mysteries of the mind and replicating them.

I specialize in human-robot cooperation, how robots can understand human goals and anticipate the need for help: learn to interact naturally with us.
